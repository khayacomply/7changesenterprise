Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for weddings and large events. However, we do accept last-minute bookings depending on availability. Contact us as early as possible to secure your date!

Do you travel outside of your main location?

Yes! We serve clients across South Africa. Travel fees may apply depending on the distance. Contact us for a customized quote based on your event location.

What is included in your event decor packages?

Our decor packages can include table settings, centerpieces, backdrops, lighting, draping, and custom design elements. Each package is tailored to your specific needs and budget. Contact us for a detailed breakdown.

Do you provide setup and teardown services?

Yes! All our rental packages include professional setup and teardown services. Our team will handle everything so you can focus on enjoying your event.

Can I customize the sublimation branding designs?

Absolutely! We work with you to create custom designs that match your brand or event theme. Send us your logo, ideas, or let our design team create something unique for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, cash, and card payments. A deposit is required to secure your booking, with the balance due before the event date. Contact us for detailed payment terms.

Do you offer package deals for multiple services?

Yes! We offer discounted package deals when you book multiple services together. Contact us to discuss your needs and we'll create a custom package that saves you money.

What happens if there's an emergency on the event day?

We have contingency plans for all our events and our team is available 24/7 during your event. We also have backup equipment ready to ensure your event runs smoothly no matter what.
